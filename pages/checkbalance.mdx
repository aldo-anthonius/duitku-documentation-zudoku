title: "Check Balance"
---

The **Check Balance API** allows you to programmatically retrieve your current available fund balance for disbursements. 

We highly recommend checking your balance before initiating large or batch transfers to ensure you have sufficient funds, thereby avoiding failed transactions.

## Common Use Cases

1. **Pre-Transfer Validation:** Call this API before executing the [Transfer Request](/transferonline#step-2-execute-transfer) to ensure your balance covers the total `amountTransfer` plus any applicable bank administrative fees.
2. **Internal Dashboard Sync:** Retrieve your real-time balance to display on your internal back-office or ERP system, allowing your finance team to monitor funds without needing to log in to the Duitku Dashboard.

---

## Check Balance Request

To check your current disbursement balance, you only need to send your basic merchant credentials.

**Endpoints:**
- `Sandbox` : POST `https://sandbox.duitku.com/webapi/api/disbursement/checkbalancesandbox`
- `Production` : POST `https://passport.duitku.com/webapi/api/disbursement/checkbalance`

### Signature Generation
To authorize the request, you must include a `signature` parameter. Generate this by hashing the following concatenated string using **SHA256**:

> `email` + `timestamp` + `secretKey`

:::tip
Always ensure you are using the correct `secretKey` specifically provided for the Disbursement feature.
:::
