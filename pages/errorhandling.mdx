Errors In Executing the Transfer
After a transfer request is successfully accepted (returning HTTP 200 or 202), it may still fail during our banking partner's processing or be rejected by the recipient bank.

It is critically important that you understand these failure codes to decide the appropriate action to take, especially regarding Retries.

Execution & Bank Errors
Below is a comprehensive list of the possible failure codes you may receive via Callback or the Check Status API, along with our suggested actions:

:::tip{title="Best Practice for Retries"}
As a golden rule, never build an automatic retry mechanism for timeouts or general errors. Only retry a transaction automatically if the error explicitly states that the request was invalid or you had insufficient balance.
:::
